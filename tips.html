Neighborhood Watch Programs:

Encourage the establishment of neighborhood
watch programs where community members
look out for each other's properties and report
suspicious activities to local authorities by
installation of CCTV's and implementing curfews.

Community Communication:

Foster open lines of communication within the
community. Establish a community forum, social
media group, or mailing list to share security-related
information, updates, and concerns.
